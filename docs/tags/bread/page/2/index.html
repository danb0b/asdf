Pizza Crust
By Dan Aukes

Ingredients 3 cups Flour 2 cups Water 2 rounded tsp / 1 packet yeast 1/2 tsp salt 1 tbsp sugar Directions Heat one cup of water to 110-120 deg F (usually ~30 seconds in the microwave) Add sugar and yeast; stir and set aside for 10 minutes to proof Mix flour and salt in a big metal bowl After ten minutes, if the yeast has become frothy, add it and one more cup water to the flour mixture Stir with a mixer for ~10 minutes.
