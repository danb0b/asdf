## Ingredients

-   4 eggs
-   1 cup milk
-   1 cup flour
-   1/4 stick butter

## Directions

1.  Preheat oven to 450 deg.
2.  Beat eggs 30 seconds
3.  Beat in milk then flour 30 seconds.
4.  Cook in oven - 8-12 min. til it looks done
5.  Enjoy with lemon & powdered sugar or jam or fruit
